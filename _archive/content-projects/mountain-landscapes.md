This series explores the dramatic mountain landscapes of the Pacific Northwest. Shot over multiple seasons, these images capture the interplay of light, weather, and terrain that make this region so compelling to photograph.

Each image in this collection tells a story of patience — waiting for the right light, the right moment when clouds and peaks align to create something extraordinary.
