Cities are full of unnoticed geometry — the repetition of windows, the sweep of a staircase, the converging lines of a corridor. This project documents these moments of accidental beauty found in everyday urban spaces.

The images were shot across several cities, focusing on the abstract qualities of architectural design rather than the buildings themselves.
